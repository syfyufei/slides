Dear Students,

I am pleased to provide you with information regarding the utilization
of our course homepage.

\begin{enumerate}
\def\labelenumi{\arabic{enumi}.}
\item
  Course Homepage Access: The course homepage can be accessed via the
  following URL:
  \url{https://adriansun.drhuyue.site/course/r-workshop-tokyo-2024.html}
\item
  Course Materials: Our course homepage serves as the central hub for
  all course materials. Here, you can access weekly lecture slides and
  exercises (HKT) in advance. We encourage you to review these materials
  prior to each session to enhance your preparation and engagement.
\item
  Individual Tutoring Sessions: For students unable to attend the
  regular course sessions, we offer flexible one-on-one tutoring
  options. You are cordially invited to utilize the scheduling widget
  located in the bottom right corner of the course homepage to arrange
  personalized tutoring sessions. These individualized learning
  opportunities are designed to accommodate your specific pace and
  requirements, ensuring a tailored educational experience.
\item
  Online Sessions: For sessions conducted online, access will be
  provided through links available on the course homepage. Please ensure
  to test your equipment and internet connection before the commencement
  of online sessions to guarantee smooth participation.
\end{enumerate}

Important Note: The third week of our course will be conducted online.

participating in online sessions, please do not hesitate to contact me.
